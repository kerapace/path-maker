This is a simple graph paper app. You can draw multiple kinds of shapes, choose to undo and redo your drawings, and even export and import your drawing as a JSON file. I created this app because I was playing in an OD&D campaign where the party was required to map everything and none of the apps I found were nearly spacious enough and had the features I wanted. So I made this, and I'm pretty happy with it.
